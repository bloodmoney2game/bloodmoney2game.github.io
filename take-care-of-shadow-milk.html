About Take Care Of Shadow Milk

Welcome to Take Care of Shadow Milk, the place to play the trending Scratch game Take Care of Your Own Shadow Milk completely free, right in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups—just click and start having fun.

What Is Take Care of Shadow Milk About?

Also known as Take Care of Your Own Shadow Milk, this virtual pet-style game is developed on the Scratch platform. It’s simple yet highly addictive. In this fan-made game by GPE_sb3, you get to interact with Shadow Milk Cookie from Cookie Run: Kingdom. The twist? Everything you do is up to you, with no strict rules.

How to Play

The game lets you engage with Shadow Milk Cookie in many ways, blending care with mischief:

  • Want to feed and take care of Shadow Milk to keep him happy? Go ahead.

  • Want to prank him or cause some chaos? That works too.

The fun part is you decide how the story unfolds.

Basic Instructions

  1. Move through rooms like the bedroom, kitchen, or bathroom.

  2. Drag and drop food, objects, or tools to see how Shadow Milk reacts.

  3. Monitor energy, hunger, health, and hygiene bars.

  4. Throw away items you don’t need by dragging them to the trash.

No missions, no game-over—just endless experimentation.
