About BLOODMONEY!

Welcome to BLOODMONEY!, a free-to-play clicker horror game that challenges your morals and limits. Developed by Shroomychrist Studios and released on August 3, 2025, this game offers a unique blend of simulation, clicker mechanics, and horror elements, all wrapped in a deceptively charming pastel aesthetic.

What Is BLOODMONEY! About?

In BLOODMONEY!, you find yourself in a dire situation: suffering from a life-threatening illness, the only way to afford the necessary $25,000 surgery is through an unexpected and unsettling opportunity. While driving home, you encounter Harvey Harvington, a man offering $1 for every click on him. The catch? Each click causes him pain, and the more you hurt him, the more money you earn. As you progress, you can purchase items from an offscreen shop to increase your earnings, but at what cost?

How to Play

  • Click Harvey: Each click earns you $1, but it also causes him pain.

  • Purchase Items: Use your earnings to buy items that increase your income per click.

  • Make Choices: Decide how much you're willing to hurt Harvey to achieve your goal.

  • Face the Consequences: Your actions lead to different outcomes, testing your moral compass.

Why It's Trending

BLOODMONEY! has gained attention for its unique gameplay and thought-provoking themes. The juxtaposition of cute, pastel visuals with dark, horror elements creates a compelling contrast that draws players in.
