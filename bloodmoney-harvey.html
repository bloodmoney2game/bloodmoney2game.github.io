About Bloodmoney Harvey

Bloodmoney Harvey is a psychological horror clicker game that tests your morality and decision-making. Developed by Shroomychrist Studios, the game combines simple clicker mechanics with a dark storyline, creating a tense yet addictive experience.

What Is Bloodmoney Harvey?

In Bloodmoney Harvey, you are in desperate need of $25,000 for a life-saving surgery. Harvey Harveyington, a mysterious figure, offers you $1 for every click—but each click causes him pain. The more you click, the more money you earn, putting you in a moral dilemma: how far are you willing to go for survival?

How to Play

  • Click to Earn: Each click on Harvey increases your money.

  • Purchase Tools: Buy items to increase your earnings per click, including syringes, scissors, and other tools.

  • Face Consequences: Your actions influence the story, leading to multiple possible endings.

Features

  • Addictive Clicker Mechanics: Simple gameplay that keeps you engaged.

  • Moral Dilemma: Decisions impact Harvey and your outcome.

  • Unique Visuals: Pastel-colored graphics contrast with dark, unsettling themes.

  • Multiple Endings: Replay the game to explore all possible outcomes.

Why Play Bloodmoney Harvey?

Players are drawn to the combination of cute yet eerie visuals with a morally challenging storyline. The game’s replayability, short session length, and emotional tension make it an unforgettable experience.
