About BloodMoney Unblocked

BloodMoney Unblocked is the unblocked version of the popular psychological horror clicker game, allowing players to experience the dark and addictive gameplay anywhere—even at school or work. Developed by Shroomychrist Studios, this game combines simple clicker mechanics with a chilling moral dilemma.

What Is BloodMoney Unblocked?

In BloodMoney Unblocked, you play as a character in desperate need of $25,000 for a life-saving surgery. Harvey Harvington appears, offering $1 for every click—but each click inflicts visible pain on him. The more you click, the more money you earn, but your actions carry serious moral consequences. This creates a gripping challenge: how far will you go for survival?

How to Play

  • Click to Earn: Click on Harvey to accumulate money.

  • Buy Tools: Use your earnings to buy items that increase your money per click.

  • Face Moral Choices: Decide how much pain you are willing to cause to reach your goal.

The game encourages replay, as each choice can affect the outcome, leading to one of multiple possible endings.

Features

  • Unblocked Access: Play directly in your browser without restrictions.

  • Multiple Endings: Your decisions shape the story’s conclusion.

  • Unique Art Style: Pastel visuals contrast with dark themes for a creepy but engaging aesthetic.

  • Immersive Audio: Original soundtrack enhances tension and atmosphere.

Why Play BloodMoney Unblocked?
